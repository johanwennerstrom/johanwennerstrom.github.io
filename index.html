Co-Lab

Record. Interact. Listen.

Make music with people you otherwise wouldn't get creative with. Choose your favorite genre and get paired with completely different genres. Get started and expand your palate by either mixing your preferences with other users or adapting entirely to something new.

Record.

Record your track directly in the browser. Have other users add tracks to start building on to the project.

Interact.

Interact with other users from vastly different musical backgrounds. Get involved in projects you otherwise wouldn't.

Listen.

Listen to your past projects, other users projects and hear your musical horizons widen!
