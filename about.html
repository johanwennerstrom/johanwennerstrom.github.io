About Co-lab

Co-lab is an attempt to bring people with different musical preferences together. Most people who make music together on a non-proffesioial level have similar musical backgrounds and preferences. This is quite natural and not inherently wrong. It is however limiting in what can be accomplished music wise. If you get the tools to meet people with different musical preferences than yourself, it will influence how your music sounds. I think that besides the music itself. Mixing people from different genres can also bring people from different sub-cultures and groups togheter.
